Instructions for the Summer Session I and II

How to prepare for registration (Virginia Tech students)

These are the steps Virginia Tech students should take to prepare for registering for classes.

Tools and ingredients

  • Computer with Internet connection
  • Hokie SPA Login information

Steps

  1. Log in to your Hokie SPA account using your PID and password.

  2. Select "Registration and Schedule" from the Hokie SPA main menu.

  3. Click "Prepare for Registration" link.

  4. Select the open term in which you are attempting to register.

  5. Click "Continue."

  6. Complete each of the pre-registration requirements that appear on the screen.

  7. Select the "Registration" tab in the navigation panel.

That is how Virginia Tech students should prepare to register for classes.

How to prepare for registration (non-Virginia Tech students)

These are the steps non-Virginia Tech students should take to prepare for registering for classes.

Tools and ingredients

  • Computer with Internet connection

Steps

  1. Apply as a Non-Degree Seeking student at www.admissions.vt.edu/apply/apply-now.html

  2. Follow the link to establish your account.

  3. Follow the link to fill out an application as a Non-Degree seeking applicant.

  4. Make corrections to the data.

  5. Save and send your application.

  6. Follow the instructions to pay the application fee online.

  7. Check your email for a message from "admissions@vt.edu" with the subject "Virginia Tech Application Status Page."

  8. Schedule a meeting with your academic advisor at your current institution.

    9. Obtain permission to receive authorization to enroll in courses at Virginia Tech and transfer the credit to your home institution.

If you are not a Virginia Tech student, this is how you should prepare to register for classes.

How to find the CRN of the class(es) you need

These are the steps for all students to find the CRN (course request number) of the class they need to take. You will need to use the CRN later in the process of registering.

Tools and ingredients

  • Computer with Internet connection
  • Hokie SPA Login information
  • Course number of the class (Ex: ENGL 3814)

Steps

  1. Log in to your Hokie SPA account using your PID and password.

  2. Click on the far-left tab in the navigation panel that says "Hokie Plus."

  3. Click on "Timetable of Classes."

  4. Locate the "Campus" field.

    5. Select "Blacksburg" to see if the class you need is offered on campus. Select "Virtual" to see if the class you need is offered online.

  5. Locate the "Term" field and select Summer I 2017 or Summer II 2017, depending on the term in which you need to take the class.

  6. Locate the "Subject" field and select the department name for the class you are taking.

  7. Locate the "Course Number" field and type in the four-digit number of the class.

  8. Click "FIND class sections."

  9. Locate the CRN number for the course.

    10. It will be all the way on the left-hand side of the course listing. This is what you will use to add the class.

That is how all students find the CRN for the class(es) they need to take.

How to register for a class

This is how all students register for a class at Virginia Tech.

Tools and ingredients

  • Computer with Internet connection
  • Hokie SPA Login information
  • CRN of the class

Steps

  1. Log in to your Hokie SPA account using your PID and password.

  2. Select "Registration and Schedule" from the Hokie SPA main menu.

  3. Click the "Register for Classes" link.

  4. Select the term you wish to register and click "Continue."

  5. Click "Ok" on the financial and registration notice that appears.

  6. Click on the second tab labeled "Enter CRNs" and put in the CRN for the class.

  7. Find the course and section you want to take and click the "Add" button to register.

These are the steps that all students should take to register for a class.
