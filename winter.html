Instructions for the Winter Session

How to drop a class

You might need to drop a class after you have begun taking it. These are the instructions for how to officially drop a class in the system.

Tools and ingredients

  • Computer with Internet connection
  • Hokie SPA Login information

Steps

  1. Log in to your Hokie SPA account using your PID and password.

  2. Select "Registration and Schedule" form the Hokie SPA main menu.

  3. Click "[Drop/Add] Register for Classes."

  4. Select the term that the class is in and click "Continue."

  5. Click the Action drop-down menu on the class you want to drop.

  6. Select "Drop Web" on the menu.

  7. Click "Submit" on the bottom of the screen.

These are the steps to take to drop a class from Winter Session.

How to pay for class(es) (Virginia Tech students)

Virginia Tech students should follow these instructions to pay for classes.

Tools and ingredients

  • Computer with Internet connection
  • Hokie SPA Login information
  • Credit Card or Bank Account information, or another form of payment

Steps

  1. Log in to your Hokie SPA account using your PID and password.

  2. Click on the far-right tab in the navigation panel that says "Hokie Spa."

  3. Click on "University Account Information."

  4. Click "View and Pay e-Bill."

  5. Locate "Make Payment" on the menu on the left side of the screen and click on it.

  6. Type in a payment amount and choose a payment method from the drop-down menu.

  7. Click "Continue."

These are the steps for Virginia Tech students to pay for classes.

How to pay for class(es) (International Students)

International students should follow these instructions to pay for classes.

Tools and ingredients

  • Computer with Internet connection
  • Hokie SPA Login information
  • Credit Card or Bank Account information, or another form of payment

Steps

  1. Visit the Virginia Tech Bursar's website at www.bursar.vt.edu.

  2. Locate the "Payment Options for International Students" option.

  3. Click on "More" and follow the instructions on the screen.

These are the steps for International students to pay for classes.

How to apply for financial aid

All students should follow these instructions to apply for financial aid.

Tools and ingredients

  • Computer with Internet connection

Steps

  1. Visit the FAFSA website at https://fafsa.ed.gov

  2. Click "Start A New FAFSA" if you haven't filled one out before. Follow the instructions to fill out the forms.

  3. Click "Login" if you have filled out a FAFSA before. Fill out the forms to apply for the Summer or Winter term.

These are the steps for all students to apply for financial aid.

How to withdraw from Winter Session

All students who need to withdraw from Winter Session at Virginia Tech completely should follow these instructions using the online resignation form.

Tools and ingredients

  • Computer with Internet connection

Steps

  1. Visit the Virginia Tech registrar's website at www.registrar.vt.edu

  2. Click on the middle tab labeled "Undergraduate Students" halfway down the page.

  3. Click on the "Forms" link.

  4. Click on the "Student Withdrawal/Resignation Form" link to access the PDF.

  5. Fill out the form and submit it to the Office of the University Registrar located in the Student Services Building at 800 Washington St., SW, Blacksburg, VA 24061.

These are the steps to withdraw from Winter Session at Virginia Tech completely.
